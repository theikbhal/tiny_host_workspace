How do I upload my site? ▼
Simply create a ZIP file containing your index.html and assets, then drag and drop it onto the upload area on the dashboard. Choose your subdomain name and click Launch!
How long does my site stay live? ▼
Free sites stay live for 7 days. Pro plan sites stay live forever. You can always re-upload to extend the duration.
Can I use a custom domain? ▼
Yes! Custom domains are available on the Pro plan. You can point your domain to your Tiiny Host site using CNAME records.
What file types are supported? ▼
We support ZIP files containing HTML, CSS, JavaScript, images, and other static assets. Your ZIP should contain an index.html file as the entry point.
Is there a file size limit? ▼
Free accounts have a 100MB limit per site. Pro accounts can upload up to 1GB per site. Enterprise plans have unlimited storage.
Can I update my site after uploading? ▼
Yes! Use the "Update Site" tab on the dashboard to upload a new version of your site to the same subdomain.
Do you support HTTPS? ▼
Yes! All sites are automatically served over HTTPS with free SSL certificates.
